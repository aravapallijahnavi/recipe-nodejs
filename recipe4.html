Fish Tacos

Fish Tacos, a culinary delight, combine the freshest catch with vibrant flavors. Succulent, grilled fish nestled in warm tortillas, adorned with crisp cabbage, zesty salsa, and a drizzle of creamy sauce. Each bite is a symphony of textures and tastes, from the tender fish to the crunch of fresh veggies. Whether enjoyed by the seaside or savored inland, Fish Tacos offer a taste of coastal bliss, bringing together the essence of the ocean and the zest of Mexican-inspired cuisine.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : NoN-veg Veg Fish Soups

instructions

steps

Season fish fillets with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Grill or pan-fry the fish until cooked through. In a bowl, mix shredded cabbage with mayonnaise and lime juice to create slaw. Warm tortillas and assemble tacos with fish and slaw. Feel free to add these recipes to your recipe app, and enjoy the delightful flavors.

ingredients

- 500g white fish fillets
1 cup shredded cabbage
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon cumin
Salt and pepper to taste
Flour tortillas
