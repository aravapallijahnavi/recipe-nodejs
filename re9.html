Lentil Soup

Lentil Soup is a comforting bowl of warmth and nourishment, showcasing the humble yet nutritious lentil. Simmered with aromatic vegetables like carrots, onions, and celery, along with savory herbs and spices, it delivers a robust flavor profile. The lentils add a hearty texture, while the broth becomes a savory elixir. Whether enjoyed as a hearty standalone meal or a cozy starter, Lentil Soup embodies simplicity and wholesomeness, making it a timeless and satisfying choice for those seeking a bowl of comfort.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : NoN-veg Veg Fish Soups

instructions

steps

In a large pot, combine lentils, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot. These vegetarian recipes are not only simple but also full of flavor. Enjoy adding them to your recipe app

ingredients

- 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
1 onion, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
2 celery stalks, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
6 cups vegetable broth
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon paprika
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh cilantro for garnish
