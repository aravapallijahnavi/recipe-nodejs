Spinach and Chickpea Curry

Spinach and Chickpea Curry is a flavorful and nutritious dish that brings together robust spices and wholesome ingredients. Tender chickpeas are simmered in a fragrant curry sauce enriched with spinach, tomatoes, and aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The result is a harmonious blend of textures and tastes, with the earthiness of chickpeas complemented by the vibrant freshness of spinach. This vegetarian delight is both comforting and nourishing, served best with rice or naan, offering a delightful journey through Indian-inspired cuisine.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : NoN-veg Veg Fish Soups

instructions

steps

In a pan, heat vegetable oil. Add onions and cook until translucent. Stir in curry powder, cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they form a thick sauce. Add chickpeas and spinach. Cook until spinach wilts and chickpeas are heated through. Season with salt and serve with rice or naan.

ingredients

- 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 onion, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped
2 tablespoons curry powder
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon coriander
1 teaspoon turmeric
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
Salt to taste
