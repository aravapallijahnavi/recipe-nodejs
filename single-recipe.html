Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani, a delectable South Asian dish, harmoniously blends fragrant basmati rice, succulent chicken pieces, and aromatic spices. Marinated chicken, slow-cooked to perfection, intertwines with seasoned rice, creating a symphony of flavors. Infused with saffron, fried onions, and mint, each bite offers a tantalizing medley of tastes. This iconic dish, celebrated for its richness and complexity, reflects the culinary diversity of the region. Served with raita or a side salad, Chicken Biryani stands as a timeless favorite, enticing palates with its spicy allure and satisfying textures.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : NoN-veg Veg Fish Soups

instructions

steps

1. Wash the basmati rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.

2. In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add soaked and drained rice, 1 teaspoon of salt, and cook until the rice is 70-80% cooked. Drain and set aside.

3. Heat oil or ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions for later use.

4. Add chicken pieces to the remaining onions in the pan. Cook until the chicken is browned.

5. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomatoes, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate.

6. Stir in yogurt, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Cook for another 5 minutes.

7. In a separate small pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add all the biryani masala ingredients and fry until fragrant. Add this mixture to the chicken curry.

8. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).

9. In a deep ovenproof dish, layer half of the partially cooked rice, followed by the chicken curry. Top with the remaining rice.

10. Sprinkle the reserved fried onions on top. Cover the dish tightly with aluminum foil or a lid.

11. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and the rice to cook completely.

12. Serve the delicious chicken biryani hot with raita or a side salad.

ingredients

- 2 cups basmati rice
- 500g chicken, cut into pieces
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 large onions, thinly sliced
- 3 tomatoes, chopped
- 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
- 2 tablespoons biryani masala
- 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
- 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
- 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
- 4 cups water
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder
- Salt to tast
