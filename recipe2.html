Chicken Curry

Chicken Biryani, a delectable South Asian dish, harmoniously blends fragrant basmati rice, succulent chicken pieces, and aromatic spices. Marinated chicken, slow-cooked to perfection, intertwines with seasoned rice, creating a symphony of flavors. Infused with saffron, fried onions, and mint, each bite offers a tantalizing medley of tastes. This iconic dish, celebrated for its richness and complexity, reflects the culinary diversity of the region. Served with raita or a side salad, Chicken Biryani stands as a timeless favorite, enticing palates with its spicy allure and satisfying textures.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : NoN-veg Veg Fish Soups

instructions

steps

Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned. Mix in curry powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the chicken is tender. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with rice or bread.

ingredients

- 500g chicken pieces
2 large onions, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, pureed
1/4 cup cooking oil
1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
2 tablespoons curry powder
1 teaspoon cumin powder
1 teaspoon coriander powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
Salt to taste
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
