Pasta Primavera

Pasta Primavera is a celebration of vibrant, seasonal vegetables harmoniously tossed with al dente pasta. Bursting with colors and flavors, this dish typically features an array of veggies like cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, zucchini, and broccoli, sautéed to perfection. The medley is then combined with a light, herb-infused sauce, creating a refreshing and wholesome pasta experience. Whether enjoyed as a vegetarian delight or paired with grilled chicken or shrimp, Pasta Primavera is a delightful ode to the freshness of the garden on a plate.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : NoN-veg Veg Fish Soups

instructions

steps

Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a pan, heat olive oil. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add mixed vegetables and sauté until they are tender-crisp. Toss in cooked pasta, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Garnish with fresh basil and serve..

ingredients

- 2 cups mixed vegetables (zucchini, cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, carrots), sliced
250g pasta
1/4 cup olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh basil for garnish
