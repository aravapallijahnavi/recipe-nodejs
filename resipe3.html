Spicy Grilled Shrimp

To make Spicy Grilled Shrimp, marinate fresh shrimp in a blend of chili powder, cayenne, paprika, garlic, and lemon juice. Allow the flavors to meld for 30 minutes. Skewer the marinated shrimp and grill over medium-high heat until they develop a smoky char, about 2-3 minutes per side. The heat from spices enhances the natural sweetness of the shrimp. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime for an irresistible dish that's quick, simple, and bursting with spicy, grilled perfection.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

3 servings

steps

Spicy Grilled Shrimp delivers a tantalizing explosion of flavors. Fresh shrimp, marinated in a fiery blend of spices, melds with smoky grill marks to create a mouthwatering delight. The heat from chili, cayenne, and paprika amplifies the savory notes, while a touch of citrus adds a zesty twist. Grilling enhances the shrimp's natural sweetness, creating a perfect balance. Served as an appetizer or main course, this dish caters to spice enthusiasts, leaving taste buds tingling and craving more of the succulent, charred goodness.

ingredients

- 500g shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Lemon wedges for serving
